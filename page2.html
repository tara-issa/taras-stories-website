Volunteering in Bali

I volunteered with IVHQ in Bali, renovating schools in Soe. It was a short, meaningful journey, working together with the local community to improve the schools. The experience was filled with shared laughter, hard work, and cultural exchange. Even though it was a brief adventure, volunteering with IVHQ in Bali made a lasting impact, emphasizing the importance of connections and making a positive difference.
